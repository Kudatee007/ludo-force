How to Play Ludo Force

Welcome to Ludo Force! Here’s everything you need to know to get started:

🔹 Objective:

Be the first team to move all 4 pieces from your base to your Home.

🔹 Game Setup:

  1. Each team has 4 tokens.
  2. Teams take turns rolling the dice.
  3. You must roll a 6 to unlock a token from base.
  4. Once unlocked, tokens follow a specific path around the board.
  5. The final stretch is the home path—unique to each team.

🔹 Dice Rules:

  1. Roll a 6 to unlock or get a bonus turn.
  2. You get one roll per turn, unless you roll a 6.
  3. If no moves are possible, the turn passes.

🔹 Movement:

  1. Click on a token to move it after rolling the dice.
  2. Tokens move based on the number shown on the dice.
  3. Tokens reaching the final spot (🏠) are safe and removed from the board.

🔹 Capturing:

  1. Landing on an opponent’s token sends it back to base (unless on a safe square).
  2. This gives your team a bonus roll.

🔹 Safe Spots:

  1. Tokens on safe spots cannot be captured.

🔹 Winning:

The first team to move all 4 tokens to Home wins the game!

🔹 Tips:

  1. Plan ahead—especially with multiple tokens on the board.
  2. Use safe spots strategically.
  3. Capturing opponents gives you an edge—go for it if it's safe!

Let the game begin 🎉
